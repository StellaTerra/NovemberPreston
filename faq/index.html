Frequently asked questions

Therapy FAQ for California Clients

Questions below cover logistics, fees, and session format. If you need anything else, you can reach out directly before scheduling.

Common questions and answers

How can we meet for sessions?

Telehealth is available statewide in California by secure video. Ecotherapy sessions in East Bay settings are available after initial sessions, and can be integrated regularly or as needed. Review East Bay ecotherapy session details for what to expect.

What are your rates?

Typical rates are $150 for a 50-minute telehealth session, $170 for a 60-minute ecotherapy session, and $250 for a 90-minute ecotherapy session.

Do you offer a sliding scale?

Yes. A limited number of sliding-scale openings are reserved based on need and availability, with priority for BIPOC, queer, trans, and low-income clients.

Do you take insurance?

Insurance is not billed directly. A superbill can be provided for out-of-network reimbursement. Please check your specific mental health benefits with your plan.

Where are you located?

The practice is based in the East Bay of the San Francisco Bay Area. Telehealth is available statewide in California, and ecotherapy sessions are offered in East Bay locations.

Still have questions?

A free 20-minute consult is the best place to discuss fit, goals, and practical details. You can also review the About page for approach and training context.
